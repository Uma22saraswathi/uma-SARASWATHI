Traditional teaching approach to education

Though we use aids and materials in class and rely on online classes in times that demand it, we believe that interacting with students personally, and teaching them values along with subjects still works the best. Our experienced teachers will work towards your ward’s bright future, along with your ward.

Library and labs.

We believe education is what helps a child discover itself, and gradually develops into a confident individual. With an all-round curriculum, Tilak Vidyalaya ensures a healthy intellectual, physical, and spiritual growth in your child. Around 300 students passing out each year in both HSC and 10th

Our school’s history

Established in the late 1800s, our school has a rich history of being part of the swadeshi movement, even. After a few location changes and name changes, the school was named after Lokmanya Tilak in the year 1947, on the day of independence. We take huge pride in our history, and still imbibe patriotism in children.
