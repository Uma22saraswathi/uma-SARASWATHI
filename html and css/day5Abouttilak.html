About us

We’re a government aided educational institution, run by an able to management. Our dedicated teachers take pride in having educated thousands of students over the years. To get in touch, you can write an email to us.Tilak Vidyalaya has been educating students for more than a century, and has been a beacon of knowledge. With a fitting motto of “Lead me from darkness to light”, our government recognized, and aided school has provided education, support, and awareness to generations of students in and around Kallidaikurichi. With a large network of grateful alumni, giving teachers and a gracious management, Tilak Vidyalaya ensures a bright future and a brilliant education for your child. We believe education is what helps a child discover itself, and gradually develops into a confident individual. With an all-round curriculum, Tilak Vidyalaya ensures a healthy intellectual, physical, and spiritual growth in your child.
