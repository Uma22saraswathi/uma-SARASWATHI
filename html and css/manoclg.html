Govindaperi is a small taluk in in Tirunelveli District of Tamil nadu State, India. The college comesa under Karisalpatti panchayat. it is located 18 KM South of the District headquaters and about 8 kms from Cheranmahadevi bus station. The college is situated on the cheranmahadevi-to-Kalakad road. Manonmaniyam Sundaranar university College, Govindaperi, is governed by manonmaniyam sundaranar University, Tirunelveli. It was established in the year 2000 in a rented building then it was shifted to a newly constructed own building in Govindaperi, were it was inaugurated by the Honourable Vice-Chancellor Dr.Cynthiya pandian on 1-3-2008. Former Member of the Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu, Mr.P.H.Pandian, donated 5.5 acres of his land for establishing the college.
